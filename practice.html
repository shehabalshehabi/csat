CSAT Practice Material

This site contains practice material for the CSAT. Here, you will currently practice material Sample Paper 1. We advise candidates to attempt to do practice papers before using these materials. You shouldn't worry if you cannot do all the questions (or even any on your first try).

This content will be updated over the coming days. The formatting will be improved and there will be several quality of life improvements such as hints being placed in spoiler tags. Later on, we will release the practice materials for paper 2. There are no plans to release answers for paper 3 at the moment.
